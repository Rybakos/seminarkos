Mars

Mars, often referred to as the "Red Planet" due to its reddish appearance, is the fourth planet from the Sun in our solar system.

It is a terrestrial planet with a diameter of about 6,779 kilometers, making it roughly half the size of Earth.

Atmosphere Orbital Period Climate
Mars has a thin atmosphere composed mainly of carbon dioxide (CO2), with traces of nitrogen and argon Mars orbits around the Sun with an average orbital period of approximately 687 Earth days, which means a Martian year is about 1.88 Earth years.
